About Us

Welcome to Atheene Inc.

Atheene is leading edge in Canada call centre technology. We provide inbound call centre and telephone answering services as well as emergency dispatch contracts to various corporate clients across Canada and throughout other regions of Canada and the USA. Our call centre operations are based in Canada, and we do not sub contract or operate offshore telephone answering services. Our well trained and accredited staff ensure a seamless interface between the customer and your company. Atheene has the added advantage of being able to provide turnkey call centre services and integrated communications with the most up to date technology available to business and consumer operations.

Business to Business or Business to Consumer, Atheene is a leader in the industry. We can help your business grow!
